ABOUT US

WELCOME TO MINDSCAPE

Who we are

Think of Headspace as your mind’s best friend. We’re here for you whenever you need us, wherever you are, helping you get through tough times and find joy in every day.

What we do

Through science-backed meditation and mindfulness tools, Headspace helps you create life-changing habits to support your mental health and find a healthier, happier you.

Why we do it

Headspace is proven to reduce stress by 14% in just 10 days. It can also help you relax your mind in minutes, improve focus, and get the best sleep ever.

Our Vision

At Mindscape, we are passionate about transforming lives through the power of yoga and meditation. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to providing exceptional classes and a supportive community to help individuals discover inner peace, balance, and personal growth.Our vision is to create a world where every individual can experience the profound benefits of yoga and meditation. We envision a society where mental and physical well-being are prioritized, leading to happier and healthier lives for all.

Our Classes

Mindscape offers a wide range of yoga and meditation classes to suit every individual's needs and goals. From gentle and restorative practices to dynamic and challenging sessions, our expert instructors curate classes that cater to beginners and advanced practitioners alike. With a focus on alignment, breathwork, and mindfulness, our classes provide a holistic experience that nurtures both body and mind.

Our Values

Transformation: We believe in the transformative power of yoga and meditation. We are committed to helping our members embrace positive change, develop self-awareness, and unlock their full potential.


Inclusivity: At Mindscape, we welcome people from all walks of life, regardless of age, fitness level, or experience. We foster an inclusive and judgment-free environment where everyone feels accepted, respected, and supported on their unique journey.


Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do. From our expert instructors to our state-of-the-art facilities, we are dedicated to delivering outstanding yoga and meditation experiences that surpass expectations.


Our Team

Behind Mindscape, there is a team of highly skilled and passionate instructors who are dedicated to guiding and inspiring our members on their wellness journey. With their expertise and compassion, they create a nurturing and uplifting atmosphere that allows individuals to explore and expand their practice.

Community Involvement

At Mindscape, we are committed to giving back to the community. We actively collaborate with local organizations to promote wellness initiatives and provide opportunities for those who may not have easy access to yoga and meditation. We believe that everyone deserves the chance to experience the transformative benefits of these practices.

Join Us

We invite you to join the Mindscape community and embark on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth. Whether you are new to yoga and meditation or a seasoned practitioner, we have the resources, classes, and support to meet you where you are and help you reach your goals.

Contact us today to learn more about our membership options, class schedules, and special events. Together, let's create positive change and transform lives through the incredible practices of yoga and meditation.

Thank you for choosing Mindscape as your partner in wellness. We look forward to being a part of your transformative journey.

About Mindscape

Headspace is part of Headspace Health, the world’s most accessible, comprehensive provider of mental health and well-being care. Headspace Health also consists of Headspace for Work and Ginger, who partner with over 2,500 companies and health plans to provide access to meditation, mindfulness, coaching, therapy, and psychiatry to their members and employees.
